Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

This is peanut butter and jelly with a twist. The kids will thank you!

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons of butter
  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Heat a griddle or skillet to 350 degrees F.
  3. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side one side of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side.
  4. Place bread slices together, so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  5. Cook on the preheated griddle until golden brown and heated through, about 4 minutes per side.
